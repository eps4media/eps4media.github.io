USB Slideshow Player

A Roku app for playing clean full-screen photo slideshows from a USB drive.

Browse folders on a USB drive connected to your Roku device, select a photo folder, customize slideshow timing and transitions, and play your photos on the TV.

Support Privacy Policy Terms of Use

Features

  • Browse USB folders directly on Roku.
  • Select a folder of JPG, JPEG, or PNG photos.
  • Play full-screen slideshows with configurable timing.
  • Choose Fill Screen or Fit Entire Photo display modes.
  • Use Cut or Soft Fade transitions.
  • Adjust Soft Fade speed: Fast, Normal, Dreamy, or Very Dreamy.
  • Shuffle photos with practical repeat avoidance.
  • Optionally play supported audio files from USB storage.
  • Save, load, and clear slideshow setup locally on the Roku device.

Local-first playback

USB Slideshow Player is designed for local USB playback. It does not require an internet connection for slideshow playback, does not use accounts, does not include advertising, and does not upload your media files.

Supported photo formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG. Optional supported audio formats: MP3, M4A, AAC, WAV.
