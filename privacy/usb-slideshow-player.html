Privacy Policy for USB Slideshow Player

Effective date: 2026-05-26

USB Slideshow Player is a local Roku slideshow app by EPS4 Media. It lets users browse photos and optional music from a USB drive connected to a Roku device.

The app is designed for local playback. It does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data.

Data collection

USB Slideshow Player does not collect personal data.

The app does not use user accounts, analytics, advertising trackers, or external network services.

Local USB media

The app reads supported photo and audio files from the USB drive selected by the user. Media files remain on the user's USB drive and are not transmitted by the app.

Supported photo formats:

  • JPG
  • JPEG
  • PNG

Supported audio formats:

  • MP3
  • M4A
  • AAC
  • WAV

Saved settings

If the user chooses Save Current Setup, the app stores slideshow settings locally on the Roku device using Roku local registry storage.

Saved settings may include selected folder paths, slideshow duration, transition choice, fade speed, photo fit mode, shuffle settings, and music on/off setting.

These settings are stored locally on the Roku device only. They are not uploaded or shared. The user can remove saved settings from inside the app by choosing Clear Saved Setup.

Network access

The app is designed for local USB playback. It does not require an internet connection for slideshow playback and does not contact external servers.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact: support@eps4media.com

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if app behavior changes, such as adding network features, accounts, analytics, advertising, or cloud services.
